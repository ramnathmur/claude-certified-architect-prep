What each HTML session includes

🔍
Per-distractor explanations After submitting, see exactly why your chosen option was wrong and why each of the other wrong options is wrong — labelled by distractor type (e.g. "Correct concept, wrong scope").
🏷️
Concept tags on every question Each question is tagged with 1–3 reusable architectural pattern names ("plan mode selection", "multi-agent handoff"). The session summary shows which concept areas you missed most.
↩️
Interactive retry mode After finishing, click "Retry Wrong Answers" to re-attempt only your missed questions in a fresh interactive sub-session — no re-run needed.
✅
QA-filtered questions Each generated session is automatically checked: are all 4 options plausible? Are wrong options distinct? Does the stem have exactly one correct answer? Questions that fail 2 of 3 checks are excluded before you see them.
📊
Live score + domain breakdown Score updates on every submission. A collapsible domain panel in the header shows your running accuracy per domain. The final summary identifies your weakest area.

Tips

→ Start here: 10 questions · Anthropic-Grade · Full Exam. This is your baseline — it tells you which domains need the most work before you invest time in them.
→ Target weak domains: Use Per Chapter mode on your lowest-scoring domain from the Launchpad courses. The app distributes questions proportionally by default; Per Chapter forces 100% focus.
→ Deduplication is built in: The app logs the first 12 words of every stem generated. Next session, those stems are injected as "do not repeat" into the prompt. You won't see the same question twice.
→ Simulate exam conditions: Choose T (time-based), enter 120 minutes → 60 questions. Anthropic-Grade, Full Exam. Treat it like the real thing — no looking things up.
→ Wrong answers are the learning: Don't skip the explanation panel. The distractor-type labels ("Anti-pattern mistaken as pattern", etc.) tell you which category of mistake to watch for on exam day.
